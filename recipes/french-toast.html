French Toast

Description:

A classic French toast recipe from the 15th century.
Did you know that French Toast did NOT originate in France?

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vannila extract (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, or more as needed.

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vannila, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot.
